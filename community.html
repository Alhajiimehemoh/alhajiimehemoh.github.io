How Technology Is Transforming Healthcare in Sierra Leone

In recent years, technology has started to play a major role in improving healthcare delivery across Sierra Leone. From digital data collection to electronic reporting systems, health facilities are gradually moving away from paper-based processes.

As an IT and Data Officer working with health facilities, I have seen firsthand how simple tools like Excel tracking sheets, digital logbooks, and centralized databases can improve accountability and reporting accuracy. Programs such as MMS distribution, CHW attendance, and ultrasound reporting are now easier to monitor when data is properly organized.

Technology helps in: reducing data errors, improving reporting speed, tracking medical supplies, and supporting better decision-making.

Challenges remain: limited internet access, lack of equipment, and insufficient IT training for staff. With continuous investment in digital skills and infrastructure, healthcare systems can become more efficient and responsive.

My goal is to continue supporting digital transformation in the health sector by providing IT support, data systems, and technical training.
